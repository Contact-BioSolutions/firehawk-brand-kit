## Brand Strategy

### Mission

We empower families, landscapers and agriculture professionals to reduce their reliance on conventional herbicides, and to be part of a regenerative movement that is restoring healthy, living soil for homes, farms, and communities.

### Brand Essence

**Naturally Powerful Weed Control.**

FireHawk Bioherbicide delivers fast, effective weed control that works with nature, rather than against it. And we do it with the knowledge of science, the wisdom of vision, and the understanding to include everyone.

### Positioning

Growers, landscapers, and home gardeners can trust FireHawk Bioherbicide for weed control that's as fast, effective, and scalable as chemical herbicides, but without the harmful residues.

**Key Benefits:**
- Starts working with visible results in hours
- Quickly biodegrades, leaving no harmful residues in the soil
- Derived from plant-based fatty acids
- Patented breakthrough colloidal delivery technology based on advanced nutritional science
- Also targets weed seeds, reducing weed regrowth and reapplications
- Supports regenerative practices that nurture healthy, living soil

### Brand Manifesto

Homes. Gardens. Communities. Agriculture. All depend on healthy, living soil. Soil that deserves responsible, regenerative care.

Controlling weeds is part of healthy stewardship of the land. But for too long, our options have ignored and damaged it. Conventional herbicides may win battles, but they lose the war.

At FireHawk we believe there is a better way. A smarter, regenerative way. A way to control what grows on our land without injuring it.

It's about trusting the science in the soil. Learning from proven concepts in nutrition, hydration, and microbiomes. It's about working with nature, rather than spite of it.

Like the legendary Australian bird for which we are named, FireHawk clears the way for new growth, sparking renewal, not destruction.

It's time to think before we spray. It's time for the power of nature. It's time for FireHawk.

**FireHawk. Naturally Powerful Weed Control**

## Visual Strategy

### Brand Colors

#### Primary Colors

#### Secondary Colors

| Color | PMS Code | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|-------|----------|-----|-----|-------|
| **PALE GREEN** | PMS 365C | `#CAE098` | R202 G222 B152 | Accent, backgrounds, soft natural feel |
| **OFF WHITE** | PMS COOL GRAY 1C | `#F7F4EE` | R247 G244 B238 | Backgrounds, subtle contrast |
| **DANDELION YELLOW** | PMS 7548C | `#FBBC00` | R251 G188 B0 | Highlights, emphasis, buttons |

#### Color Usage Guidelines

**Lead with the primary palette:**
- FireHawk Orange, White, and Deep Green

**Support with the secondary palette:**
- Pale Green, Off White, and Dandelion Yellow

**Color Contrast:**
- ✓ White text on FireHawk Orange
- ✓ FireHawk Orange text on white
- ✓ White text on Deep Green
- ✓ Deep Green text on Pale Green
- ✓ Deep Green text on Off-White

**Do Not:**
- ✗ Green text on orange (affects readability)
- ✗ Yellow for large areas (highlights only)
- ✗ Orange on cream/off-white (use white for modern feel)
- ✗ Low contrast color pairings
- ✗ Overuse of colors (keep it simple)

**Font Hierarchy:**
- **Headlines:** Roboto Condensed - Black (900)
- **Sub-headings:** Roboto Serif - Medium (500)
- **Body Text:** Roboto - Regular (400), Italic, Bold (700) & Black (900)

#### Core Fonts (Professional Use)

For print and professional design work:
- **Headlines:** Acumin Pro Condensed - Black & Regular
- **Captions & Call-outs:** Value Serif Pro - Medium
- **Body Text & Subheadings:** Acumin Pro - Regular, Italic, Bold & Black

#### Typography Principles

1. **Legibility is top priority**
2. **For short headlines:** Use Acumin Pro Condensed Black in ALL CAPS
3. **For longer headlines:** Use Acumin Pro Black in sentence case
4. **Clear hierarchy:** Ensure headings are distinct from body text

### Logo

#### Logo Variants

1. **Primary Logo** - Hawk asset + wordmark (preferred)
2. **Wordmark** - FireHawk Bioherbicide text only
3. **Hawk Asset** - Hawk symbol only (supporting use)

**Always use the primary logo where possible.** If restricted with vertical space, use the wordmark.

#### Minimum Size

- **100px wide** (digital)
- Maintain clearspace equivalent to the letter 'F'

#### Logo Color Variations

**Primary logos:**
1. White on FireHawk Orange (preferred)
2. FireHawk Orange on white

**Secondary logos:**
3. Deep Green on Pale Green
4. Deep Green on White

#### Logo Don'ts

- ✗ DO NOT create new lock-ups or change proportions
- ✗ DO NOT re-create logos from fonts
- ✗ DO NOT change logo colors
- ✗ DO NOT apply to backgrounds without sufficient contrast

### Graphic Shapes

FireHawk's graphic language takes inspiration from nature:

**Design Principles:**
1. **Soft, swooping curves** - Inspired by the hawk's wings (natural, approachable)
2. **Sharp corners** - Inspired by the hawk's talons (performance, power)

**Graphic Assets:**
- **Chevrons** - Draw attention downwards, hold powerful messages
- **Rounded Holding Shapes** - Mix of rounded and sharp corners
- **Icons** - Balance of curves and sharp points with chisel detailing
- **Wing Graphics** - Target weed imagery, support performance messaging
- **Badges** - Straightforward and legible with optional chisel detail

### Photography

#### Guidelines by Audience

**Regenerative Agriculture:**
- Horticulture, orchards, vineyards
- Images of chickens, cows, healthy soil
- Ranchers overseeing healthy cover crops
- Avoid industrial crops, boom sprays, aerial application

**Home & Garden:**
- Manicured modern homes preferred
- Natural looking family imagery
- Avoid heavy emphasis on pets and small children playing on grass

**Landscaping:**
- Ensure people wear proper PPE (gloves, glasses)
- Professional settings

**Critical:** Photography must always reflect product usage directions and safety requirements.

## Verbal Strategy

### Brand Personality

**Primary Archetype: HERO**

FireHawk Bioherbicide champions families, communities, farms and the planet by empowering fast, effective weed control that protects healthy, living soils.

**Secondary Archetypes:**
- **Hero + Caregiver** for serving homes and families
- **Hero + Ruler** for the vision and control of landscaping
- **Hero + Sage** for the science mastery of agriculture

### Tone of Voice

#### Core Attributes

| Attribute | We Are... | We Are NOT... |
|-----------|-----------|---------------|
| **Confident** | Conversational, clear, honest, bold | Arrogant, vague, pretentious, condescending |
| **Empathetic** | Understanding, welcoming, caring | Simplistic, fluffy, fear-mongering |
| **Visionary** | Passionate, inspiring, forward-thinking | Melodramatic, overbearing, judgmental |
| **Knowledgeable** | Smart yet accessible, science-backed | Pompous, overly technical, boring |

#### Tone Principles

1. **Stay sharp** - Short, declarative sentences. Say more with less.
2. **Make it clear** - Everyday language first, technical terms second.
3. **Own it** - Say it like it is. Don't beat around the bush.
4. **Be smart** - Connect to science and why it matters.
5. **Be compelling** - Pull people in with passion, don't push.

**Point of View:** Second person ("you," "your," "yours")
Speak directly to the reader, making them the central figure.

#### Voice Examples

**Before:** "Introducing a paradigm shift in weed control."
**After:** "Win back your garden with fast, effective weed control and no harmful residues."

**Before:** "The mode of action for FireHawk Bioherbicide is desiccation by dehydration."
**After:** "FireHawk controls glyphosate-resistant weeds without leaving lingering residues—restoring yields, opening markets, and supporting healthy, living soils."

### Messaging Matrix

#### All Audiences

**Core Message:** Naturally Powerful Weed Control
FireHawk Bioherbicide delivers fast, effective, non-selective weed control and leaves no harmful residues.

**Key Points:**
- Very fast acting (visible results in hours)
- Biodegrades rapidly (no harmful residues)
- Weeds stay gone longer
- Targets weed seeds (reduces regrowth)
- Plant-based fatty acids
- Patented colloidal delivery technology
- Medium price point (higher than conventional, lower than organic)

#### Audience-Specific Messaging

**Home & Garden (Sarah - Caregiver Hero)**
- Voice: Protective, reassuring, warm
- Hook: "Take back your garden with weed control that's fast, effective, and biodegradable."
- Focus: Family safety, effectiveness, ease of use

**Landscaping & Municipal (Mike - Ruler Hero)**
- Voice: Authoritative, decisive, dependable
- Hook: "Fast-acting, biodegradable weed control for professionals who need results, reputation, and value."
- Focus: Performance, compliance, competitive advantage

**Agriculture (William - Sage Hero)**
- Voice: Knowledgeable, technical, responsible
- Hook: "Reclaim yields from glyphosate-resistant weeds and access clean produce markets."
- Focus: ROI, soil health, market access, science

## Usage Guidelines

### Copy Guidelines

#### Forbidden Terms (EPA Regulations)

**Cannot Say:**
1. ✗ "Toxin-free" or "Non-toxic" → ✓ CAN say "alternative to toxic herbicides"
2. ✗ "Glyphosate-Free" (US & AUS regulations)
3. ✗ "Safe for pets, people, environment" (any product that kills vegetation cannot use "safe")
4. ✗ "Organic" (US only; AUS CAN say "Organic")
5. ✗ "All-natural" → ✓ CAN say "derived from naturally occurring ingredients" or "in harmony with nature"

**Additional Guidelines:**
- Do not mention competitor products by name
- Focus on our benefits, not competitor criticism
- Always back claims with science where possible
